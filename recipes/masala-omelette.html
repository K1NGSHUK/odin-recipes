Masala Omelette

Description:

Masala omelette is a popular Indian dish available in every Indian food stall around Malaysia.

Very easy and fast to make, for breakfast or as a side dish. This unfolded omelette with crispy, bronzed edges and a soft middle is so delicious even on its own.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium red onions
  • 1 large yellow onion
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon rock salt
  • 1 pinch ground white pepper
  • 5 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps:

  1. Dice red and yellow onions into 3 different sizes.
  2. Combine eggs, onions, chili powder, turmeric, rock salt, and pepper in a large bowl; mix until well combined.
  3. Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat until shimmering.
  4. Pour in egg mixture, making sure it's evenly distributed in the skillet.
  5. Lower heat to medium, cover, and cook for 1 minute.
  6. Cut omelette into 8 pieces and continue to cook until egg starts to brown around the edges and appears to have set.
  7. Carefully flip with a spatula and cook for 2 more minutes.
